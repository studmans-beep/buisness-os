# Email Intelligence

## Purpose

Read incoming emails, understand what the customer wants, investigate connected systems, and prepare the correct response.

---

# Workflow

## Step 1

Receive email.

Example:

Hi Scott,

Did all three deliveries from yesterday arrive?

---

## Step 2

Understand the customer's intent.

Intent detected:

Delivery status enquiry

---

## Step 3

Search connected systems.

Search:

- TMS
- Delivery status
- PODs
- Driver notes

---

## Step 4

Gather evidence.

Example:

Load 1 - Delivered 09:14

Load 2 - Delivered 09:46

Load 3 - Delivered 10:03

All PODs received.

---

## Step 5

Prepare response.

Draft:

Hi,

Yes, all three deliveries completed successfully yesterday.

Please find the delivery times below...

Kind regards,

---

## Step 6

Ask for approval.

Buttons:

Approve

Edit

Reject

---

# Future

The platform should eventually send low-risk emails automatically once approved by company policy.
