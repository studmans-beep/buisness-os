# Vision

## Mission

Build the operating system that understands how a business works and completes work on behalf of its users.

---

## Vision

To become the single workspace every business uses to run its operations by connecting all of its systems, understanding every request, and completing work automatically.

---

## Problem

Businesses waste thousands of hours switching between multiple systems.

Examples include:
- Outlook
- Teams
- WhatsApp
- TMS
- CRM
- Excel
- Shared Drives

Employees spend more time finding information than acting on it.

---

## Solution

One workspace.

Users simply ask for what they need.

The platform investigates connected systems, gathers the information, and completes the work.

---

## Core Principles

1. Investigate before responding.
2. Never guess.
3. Complete work, don't just answer questions.
4. Keep humans in control of approvals.
5. One workspace for every business.
